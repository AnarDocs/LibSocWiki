**Silvia Rivera Cusicanqui** (1949) is an
[indigenist](Indigenism.md "wikilink"), [anarchist](Anarchism.md "wikilink"),
[feminist](Feminism.md "wikilink") and historian.

## Life

### Early Life

She was born in La Paz to parents of indigenous descent.
