---
title: Russophobia
permalink: /Russophobia/
---

**Russophobia** or **Anti-Russian Sentiment** refers to
[racism](racism.md "wikilink"), prejudice, negative feelings and/or
discrimination against [Russian](Russia.md "wikilink") people or people of
Russian descent.
