![](Carribean.jpg "Carribean.jpg") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in Caribbean that are relevant to [libertarian
socialism](Libertarian_Socialism.md "wikilink").

- 1892: [University of Havana
  Strike](University_of_Havana_Strike_(1892).md "wikilink") (Cuba)
- 1903: [Trinidad and Tobago Water
  Riots](Trinidad_and_Tobago_Water_Riots_(1903).md "wikilink")
- 1953: [Cuban Revolution](Cuban_Revolution.md "wikilink")
- 1994: [Maleconazo](Maleconazo_(1994).md "wikilink") (Cuba)
- 2006: [Aruba Oilworkers'
  Strike](Aruba_Oilworkers_Strike_(2006).md "wikilink") (Caribbean
  Netherlands)
