[state](List_of_States "wikilink") in Eastern Europe, bordering
[Germany](Germany "wikilink"), [Sweden](Sweden "wikilink"),
[Russia](Russia "wikilink"), Lithuania, Belarus, Ukraine, Slovakia and
Czechia.
