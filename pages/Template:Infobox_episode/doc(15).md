## Description

To use this template, copy the following code and fill in the
appropriate fields.

## Syntax

    {{infobox episode
     | title             =
     | partOfSeason      =
     | episodeNumber     =
     | image             = [e.g. "Example.jpg"]
     | airdate           =
     | writer            =
     | director          =
     | previousEpisode   =
     | nextEpisode       =
    }}

## Sample output

    {{infobox episode
     | title             = Flower of Babel
     | partOfSeason      = One
     | episodeNumber     = Three
     | image             = Example.jpg
     | airdate           = Unaired
     | writer            = D. Righta
     | director          = A. di Rektour
     | previousEpisode   = Episode Two
     | nextEpisode       = Episode Four
    }}
