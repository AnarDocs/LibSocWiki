The **Republic of Indonesia** is a capitalist
[state](List_of_States.md "wikilink") in Southeast Asia, bordering
[Malaysia](Malaysia.md "wikilink"), [Singapore](Singapore.md "wikilink"),
[Timor-Leste](Timor-Leste.md "wikilink"), [Papua New
Guinea](Papua_New_Guinea.md "wikilink") and
[Australia](Australia.md "wikilink").
